Donji Vakuf

Me

This is the traveller in question, me, Yusuf Kavranoglu, photographed in different countries to prove that I like travelling.

Places

I was just passing-by this city, but then saw a historical castle in Google Maps in a very remote part of the city, which later I learned to be the historical city. The castle was not marked on the map, but I knew of its existence because some people had posted photoes of it for other locations on Google Maps.

After some searching though, I was able to find it. It was on top of a mountain, hence very snowy. Also, it was free of any human footprints while being near a cliff, so there was a danger of stepping on an unsafe surface and falling down. Luckily there were some dog footprints leading to the castle walls, which allowed me to take a relatively safer path.

People

In the historical town, an old lady, despite my lack of the Bosnian language, was able to understand me and direct me towards the castle.
