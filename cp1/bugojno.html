Places and people

I was going through Bugojno, on my way to Jajce. I stopped on "Kulturni centar princeze Dževhere", which is essentially a mosque, to pray. On my way out, met Yusuf Sipahić, an old gentleman, who said he was ethnically Bosnian, however his ancestor who he got his last name from was actually Turkish, who came from Istanbul to Bosnia centuries ago.

His other grandparent bared the last-names, Begović and Duraković. The name Begović, together with Sipahić, leaves no doubt that he had at least some nobility from his ancestors.
